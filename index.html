TXT, also known as TOMORROW X TOGETHER, is a 5-member boy group formed by Big Hit Music (part of HYBE Labels). The group consists of: Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun and Huening Kai. They debuted on March 4th, 2019 with their first mini-album 'The Dream Chapter: STAR'. There are no set positions within the group, it changes each comeback/song.

TOMORROW X TOGETHER are five buys who came together under one dream in hopes of building a better tomorrow
